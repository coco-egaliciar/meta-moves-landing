MetaMoves is a collection of unique motion capture data files living as NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain, that began in late January 2022, and it is the first NFT project of its kind. MetaMoves are adaptable to any T-pose avatar, can be used several times, and are Metaverse friendly.

The project had released 2 small collections of 1:1 movements already, both times the collection sold out in less than 30 minutes.
