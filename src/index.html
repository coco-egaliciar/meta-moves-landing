Social Cause

MetaMoves is not just committed to bringing life through movement for NFTs, but also cares about carrying that ethos forward in real life. We have developed a comprehensive pay it forward strategy which includes donating 10% of every 1ETH made by a MetaMove mint and organizing impact auctions for the community to participate in - all proceeds will be donated to people and pets in need of prosthetics.

We will be accepting applications, evaluating and proceeding with as many cases as we can throughout the year. For this purpose, we're partnering with MediPrint, a company led by @ElHombreRobot, who designs and prints in 3D robotic arms and legs controlled by the human brain, utilizing recyclable materials, portable electroencephalography, and artificial intelligence.

About us

MetaMoves is the first NFT of its kind. It is based in Mexico with a team of passionate creators who believe that motion is one of the most important components of our digital identity.

We help avatars communicate themselves through movement, expressing their thoughts, feelings, and strengths. Everyone on the team, from freelancers to the leadership to collaborators around the globe, shares the Web3 spirit of innovation, collaboration, and community.

MetaMoves represents the beginning of a new movement that is about to explode!
