Social Cause

MetaMoves is committed to bringing life through movement not only for NFTs, but for humans and pets too. To that end, we are partnering with MediPrint, a Mexican 3D printer company that creates robotic limbs which are controlled by the human mind using portable electroencephalography and AI. The financial aspect is covered by MetaMoves, allocating 2% of annual revenue to the cause and the community, by participating in dedicated drops organized to increase awareness and raise funds.
