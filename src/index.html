Meta-Moves

Meta-Moves is a stimulating collection of unique 1/1 animated and sound designed data files living on the Ethereum blockchain. Your Meta-Moves not only are digital art and sound, but also exclusive utility animations for your Metaverse Avatars and NFT's. All Meta-Moves holders are granted permission to access exclusive behind the scenes content as well as live input in the design process of each animated piece.

Take your NFT´s to the next level.

The purchase of an NFT from this collection grants the buyer the full rights to the Motion Capture data, which can be used in the future to produce 3D Videos, Videogames, or even animations for avatars in the metaverse. The possibilities and potential applications are limitless.

  • Adaptable to any T-pose avatar
  • Metaverse Friendly
  • Looped animations

Hand Crafted Visuals

Each one of our pieces is created individually by our highly creative team of cgi artists, engaging emotions and feelings through vibrant eye candy, the devil´s in the details.

Custom Audio Engineering

Our pieces are conceived to be coherent both visually and sonorously, sound is designed and mastered specifically for your piece.
Motion Capture Utility
We capture not only movement, but the essence of our performers in each piece transforming them in timeless custom animations for your metaverse avatars.
